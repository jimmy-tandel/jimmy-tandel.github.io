Homework 6 - Number Processing, Text Search & Phone Validation

Part 1 - Number Processing: A form where users can enter numbers with at least 4 decimal places. The application performs various mathematical operations including rounding, square root calculation, and decimal place formatting.

Part 2 - Character Search: A text analysis tool allowing users to input a large text content and search for specific characters, displaying occurrence counts and providing feedback through popup windows.

Part 3 - Phone Number Validation: A form that validates phone numbers using regular expressions to ensure they match the format (999) 999-9999, providing appropriate feedback messages to users.
